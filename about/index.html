Introduction about Me

  • Hi! I am aoi, an undergraduate student from Wuhan University, majoring in computer science.

  • I love robots, brain-computer interface, artificial intelligence, and all the other cyberpunk stuff.

  • I’m a superfan of sci-fi animation, such as GUNDAM, Cowboy Bebop, and Ghost in The Shell, which has made me passionate about robotics, computer science, and neuroscience.

  • During my college years, I’ve done lots of meaningful things: I learned harmonica, learned Japanese, took part in a robot competition, and won a very good prize.

  • Now I’m working as a research intern in a computer vision lab. My research topic is image-to-image translation(GAN-based).

  • I think ML, CV, NLP, and other AI technologies are advanced and powerful tools to push robotics and neuroscience forward.

  • My dream is to apply AI technology to robotics and neuroscience so that I can bring some fantastic things from sci-fi animes into reality.
